Dangers of Climate Change

Climate Change

Impact

Human beings and wild animals are facing new challenges to survive due to climate change.The impacts of climate change in different parts of society are all related.. Droughts can harm food production and human health.Flooding can lead to disease spread and damages to our ecosystems.Human health issues can increase mortality, impact food availability, and limit human jobs.Due to the increase in carbon dioxide produced the planets atmosphere is getting hotter which is warming the earth.

Solutions

Their are different ways we can reduce climate change driving less, walk, bike, or take public transportation more often. You'll save one pound of carbon dioxide for every mile you don't drive. Planting a single tree can help a lot by reducing one ton of carbon dioxide in its lifetime.Turning off electronics when you're not using them, will save you thousands of pounds of carbon dioxide a year.

Donations

Their are many different organizations fighting climate change at the moment some of the ones where you can donate and help improve the quality of our earth.For example climatefoundation is gathering money to restore the warm water through wave- and solar-powered deep water pumps that sustain conditions beneficial for forest growth.
